# Data Notice

This public-style paper package contains derived summaries, selected CSV tables,
and manuscript sources only.

It does not redistribute raw HST/FITS files, full local virtual environments, or
large intermediate modeling products. Those materials are outside the compact
Paper-1-style reproducibility packet and are not needed to audit the manuscript's
claim boundary.

The package's key claim is negative/conservative: no real-data T2 sampling is
authorized because no public no-contact target passed the evidence-grade
time-delay image/model product gate.
